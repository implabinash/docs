### **MONDAY (3 posts)**

**Post 1: Main narrative tweet**

- **Time: 7:00 PM EST** (5:30 AM IST Tuesday)
- **Type:** Building in public story, user insight, or vulnerability
- **Why:** US evening wind-down, high scroll time
- **Example:** "Just talked to 5 users today. 3 said the same thing..."

**Post 2: Value/insight tweet**

- **Time: 9:00 AM EST** (7:30 PM IST Monday)
- **Type:** Quick lesson, tip, or observation
- **Why:** US morning commute/coffee scroll
- **Example:** "Lesson from Week 1: Users don't care about features..."

**Post 3: Engagement tweet**

- **Time: 1:00 PM EST** (11:30 PM IST Monday)
- **Type:** Question, poll, or discussion starter
- **Why:** US lunch break
- **Example:** "Question for founders: Do you track vanity metrics or impact metrics?"
